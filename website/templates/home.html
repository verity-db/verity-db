Why VerityDB?

Built for healthcare, finance, and legal industries where compliance isn't optional.

  • Immutable by Design

    Every write is appended to a hash-chained log. Data cannot be silently modified or deleted. Full audit trail from genesis.

  • Verifiable Reads

    Cryptographic proofs accompany every read. Validate that data hasn't been tampered with, even after decades of operation.

  • Compliance-First

    Built for HIPAA, SOX, GDPR, and beyond. Multi-tenant isolation, data classification, and retention policies are core features.

  • Dual-Hash Cryptography

    SHA-256 for compliance-critical paths. BLAKE3 for high-performance internal operations. The right algorithm for each job.

  • Functional Core

    Pure, deterministic state machine at the heart. All IO at the edges. Testable, predictable, and correct by construction.

  • Open Source

    Apache 2.0 licensed. Audit the code yourself. Trust through transparency, not through obscurity.
